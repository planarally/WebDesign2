How You Help Us

2020 was a shock to everyone's system. What it demonstrated more than ever, especially in conjunction with the Black Lives Matter protests, is that it is more important than every to reach out to all memebers of our community to ensure everyone has an equal shot at safety and stability. Our goal at H.I.R.E is to help reintegrate previously-incarcerated people back into the community of OC. With no federal or state funding, our work depends on the generosity of individuals and foundations.

As a recently-founded company, our main goals are to build up community advocacy and engagement, and strengthening our team. Through your donation, we can:

  • Refine and perfect our program operations
  • Invest in strategic partnerships
  • Strengthen our relationship with our funders
  • Design in-depth voluteer education
  • Provide clients with the most effective and impactful programming

The impact of even the smallest donation can be measured, and our 100% volunteer-run organization will help get supplies to those who need them most.

  • $5 - Paper and pencils for a month of the program
  • $8 - Cost of shipping a box of parole clothing
  • $30 - Providing a funded transportation card
  • $35 - Backpacks with toiletries, snacks, other essentials
  • $40 - A suitcase and a wallet
  • $70 - Prepaid phone for a month
  • $100 - A set of interview clothes
