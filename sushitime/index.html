Reviews

CeeCee M. ★★★★★

This is my absolute favorite sushi spot in LA. They've been here for 14 years in the same little strip mall while everything around has built up and changed and places with much deeper pockets have come and gone while this place remains. Great staff and the lovely Toshi who has withstood back injury and some serious bs from the city to keep on keeping on. Sushi is fresh and creative, smooth jazz playing and a sweet mom and pop atmosphere make it a true LA gem.

John S. ★★★★★

Excellent sushi, excellent service. It is a small owner/operator (4 seats at the sushi bar and maybe 8 tables) restaurant and Chef Toshi consistently produces fresh great tasting sushi. If there is a wait, it's worth it! The prices are extremely good for the quality of food. Fun fact: Toshi is an enormous jazz fan (and guitarist himself) and there is always great music playing -ranging from classic jazz to The Rolling Stones or Jimi Hendrix! Beverage fact: they carry my favorite Echigo beer!

Francesca S. ★★★★★

This is one of my favorite sushi restaurants. Great service, fantastic sushi. It is the most authentic sushi I've had around the area. I always sit right at the bar and love to watch the sushi chefs create their masterpieces. It's such a joy to experience. Sometimes there is a long wait, but it's worth it to me!
